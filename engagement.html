Public Scholarship & Outreach

Niti Vichaar, India · 2018–Present

I lead the social policy and inequity portfolio at Niti Vichaar, an independent research and training initiative. I developed and led a certificate training programme in social science research methods — both in-person and online, for 4 cohorts.

Public Lecture · Kolkata · 25 August 2019

On Consent: The Connection Between Gender Justice and Democracy

A public lecture examining consent as both a legal and political concept — arguing that without genuine consent, neither relationships nor democracies can function. Delivered for Niti Vichaar in association with Catalyst Learning Services, Kolkata.

Workshop · Loreto College, Kolkata · August 2019 · ~85 attendees

The Methods of Conflict Resolution

An interactive workshop on conflict resolution techniques and strategies, using group activities to simulate real-life conflict scenarios. Participants practised effective bargaining skills, intervention strategies, and techniques to prevent conflicts from escalating. Delivered under Niti Vichaar / Catalyst Learning Services.

Workshop · Women's Christian College, Kolkata · 21 September 2019 · 40 students + 5 teachers

The Methods of Conflict Resolution

A one-day interactive workshop at the College Chapel, organised by the Placement & Career Counselling Cell. Participants were divided into small groups to simulate conflict scenarios — practising bargaining skills, intervention strategies, and techniques to prevent escalation. Held in collaboration with Catalyst Learning Services.

Public Lecture · Online

Know Your Course in Social Sciences: Peace and Conflict Studies

A public lecture for prospective students of the MA in Peace and Conflict Studies at TISS, produced in association with Catalyst Learning Services. The lecture introduces the discipline, its methods, and its possibilities for students considering postgraduate study in the social sciences.
