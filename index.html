I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Ireland India Institute, Dublin City University. My work examines social movements, feminist activism, and the politics of citizenship — with a particular focus on India, South Asia, and transnational feminist organising.

My research asks how ordinary people — especially women and marginalised communities — claim political voice, build solidarity, and reimagine what belonging and care can mean in contested democratic spaces. I draw on ethnographic fieldwork, archival methods, and feminist theory.
